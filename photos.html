Pastor Luther A. Werth

Meet Our Pastor Luther A. Werth

Pastor Werth was born and raised in Wisconsin. He attended Concordia Junior College in St. Paul MN, and received his AA in 1959. He then went on to receive his BA from COncordia Senior college inn Ft. Wayne, IN. Eventually to go on and receive his MDiv in 1965 from Concordia Seminary in St. Louis, MI, and ultimately his D.Min from Fuller Seminary in Pasadena, CA. in 1990. He is married to Norma Moerer, also a graduate of Concordia College. They have 3 children. He first became the pastor of a mission congregation in Tiffin Ohio, then pastor of many churches including, Timothy Lutheran in OCuncil Bluffs, IA., Hope Lutheran in Shawnee Mission, KS., Christ Our Savior Lutheran, in Livorna, MI. In retirement, he has been the interim pastor in Michigan from 2007-2011 and in California from 2012-2019. He is the Chairman of Pastoral Conference, and on various committee's in Iowa. He is the Vice President of Michigan Dist. And on many boards and committees for over 25 years in Michigan.
